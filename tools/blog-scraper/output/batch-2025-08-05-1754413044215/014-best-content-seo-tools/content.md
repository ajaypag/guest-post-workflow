					<p>But SEO today is vastly different from what it used to be. Stuffing your content with keywords and adding backlinks left and right will not get you the results.</p>
<p>SEO, as it exists today, is a sophisticated technique that is influenced by the audience’s behavior. It is an amalgamation of both on-page and off-page tactics that help you rank higher on SERPs. And there are some great tools to help you with that.</p>
<p>In this article, we’ll be covering some excellent on-page and off-page<a href="https://narrato.io/blog/15-best-seo-tools-for-content-planning/"> SEO software</a> that can help you achieve your growth objectives.</p>
<h2><span id="what-is-on-page-and-off-page-seo"><b>What is on-page and off-page SEO?</b></span></h2>
<p>A well-rounded SEO strategy comprises on-page SEO and off-page SEO. Think of them as two sides of the same coin. With on-page SEO techniques, you’ll be optimizing those sections (of your website) that you have control over.</p>
<p>The focus of on-page SEO is on producing good content with keyword optimization, the right title tags, and more. Utilizing an <a href="https://www.serpple.com/on-page-seo-checker/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">on-page SEO checker</a> can help ensure that your website meets best practices by analyzing key factors like meta tags, headings, and content structure.</p>
<p>With off-page SEO, on the other hand, the focus is on increasing your website’s authority on the search engine results pages (SERPs) through various measures. Off-page SEO consists of link building, link exchange, and other techniques.</p>
<p>Both of these techniques are important for the success of an SEO campaign.</p>

Citation Flow gives you a number predicting the influence of a URL after checking a number of connected websites.
